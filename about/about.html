About

Hello! I'm Nathan or "Woomy World" online. I am a Lego fan turned Lego designer living in Denmark. While I now build as a full time job, I still enjoy MOC building, as this engages an artistic side of Lego free from the constraints as a designer. This blog is a place for me to go a bit more in depth on these MOCs and discuss the art process behind them. Thanks for stopping by!
